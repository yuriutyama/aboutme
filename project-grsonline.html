Project Overview

The application developed for Grupo RS aims to assist the company's employees in logging their digital attendance and recording photos of the cleaning activities performed. With the app, employees can log their attendance quickly and easily using a mobile device. In addition, they can take pictures of the areas that have been cleaned and send them to the platform, allowing the supervisory team to verify and evaluate the quality of the services provided.

The app also offers productivity monitoring features, allowing managers to monitor employee performance and identify opportunities for improvement. Furthermore, the app enables employees to receive notifications and relevant information directly on their mobile devices, streamlining internal communication within the company.
